🍂 Crunchy Leaves — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Crunchy Leaves is a simple, kid-friendly sound app. We care about your privacy, and we have built the app so that it does not need any of your information.

Information we collect

None. Crunchy Leaves does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal data.

Internet and tracking

The app works fully offline. It does not connect to the internet, does not use analytics or advertising, and does not track you across apps or websites. All of the leaf sounds are generated on your device.

Children's privacy

Crunchy Leaves is designed to be safe for children. Because the app collects no data at all, it collects no data from children. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, no external links, and no social features.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact
