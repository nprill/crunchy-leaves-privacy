Frequently asked

I don't hear any sound. Make sure your device isn't muted and the volume is up. The app plays even when the silent switch is on, but the volume still needs to be turned up. You can also turn the background wind on or off in Settings (the gear in the corner).

Does the app need the internet? No. Crunchy Leaves works completely offline — all of the sounds are made right on the device.

Is it safe for my child? Yes. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, no links out of the app, and no data is collected. See our Privacy Policy for details.
